The Gem Game!

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. Click on a gem to add a specific amount of points to your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number. If your score goes above the random number you lose.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

The gems will change value for each new game.
